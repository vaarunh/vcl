Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the answers to some frequently asked questions about the session.

  • Are there any tech requirements?

    Participants need a computer or device with internet access and an email address. You listen through your computer, so functioning speakers or headphones are needed.

  • How do I ask a question during the live webinar? Will my question be answered during the presentation?

    During each live webinar you can ask a question by typing it into the Q&A box located in the control panel in ZOOM. Questions are answered at the end of the webinar, for approximately 15 minutes. We will do our best to answer as many questions as possible.

  • Once the webinar is over, will I have access to the materials and recording?

    Virtual Cyber Labs is proud to offer delegates the opportunity to watch recordings via the On Demand platform. Participants can watch and interact with these recordings at a time most convenient to them within 2 hours after the Webinar has taken place.

  • How long is a webinar?

    All of our webinars are 60 minutes which consists of a 45 minute presentation/discussion and a 15 minute Q & A session.

  • What if I have questions about registration, attending, etc?

    Virtual Cyber Labs team is available to answer your questions. Please call (+91) 82951 03548, or email support@virtualcyberlabs.com or contact us via the live support bot.
